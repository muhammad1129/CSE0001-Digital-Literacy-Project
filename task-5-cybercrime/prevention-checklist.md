Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)

✅ Prevention Tips

1) Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (avoid using the same password everywhere).
2) Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important apps like email, banking, and social media.
3) Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like logging into bank accounts.
4) Be cautious of unknown links, emails, or messages—they may be phishing attempts.
5) Keep your social media accounts private and limit what strangers can see.
6) Do not share OTP, passwords, or personal details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
7) Regularly update your apps and devices to stay protected from security threats.
8) Only download apps from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store.
   
💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

1) Never approve unknown UPI collect requests or payment requests from strangers.
2) Always verify the receiver’s name and UPI ID before sending money.
3) Avoid scanning random QR codes shared on social media or messages.
4) Do not trust offers that promise easy money, cashback, or prizes—they are often scams.

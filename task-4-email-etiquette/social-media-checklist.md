Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

✅ Do’s
1) Think before posting anything online.
2) Keep your personal information private.
3) Use respectful and polite language in posts and comments.
4) Verify information before sharing it.
5) Maintain a positive and professional online presence.

❌ Don’ts
1) Do not share fake news or unverified content.
2) Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or harmful content.
3) Do not overshare personal details like location or phone number.
4) Avoid engaging in online arguments or negativity.
5) Do not waste excessive time on social media during study hours.
